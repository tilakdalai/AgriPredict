About Our Project

Empowering Agricultural Communities

Our project addresses a critical challenge in the agricultural sector: price volatility in essential commodities like pulses, onions, and potatoes. Small-scale farmers often face uncertainty about when to sell their produce to maximize returns.

Using advanced machine learning techniques, we've developed prediction models that analyze multiple factors including historical price data, seasonal patterns, weather conditions, supply chain disruptions, and market demand.

The goal is to provide farmers, traders, and policymakers with accurate price forecasts that can help stabilize markets and improve income security for agricultural communities.

Our AI-ML Model

Data Collection

Compiled 10+ years of historical price data from agricultural markets across India, along with weather patterns, import/export figures, and production statistics.

Data Preprocessing

Cleaned and normalized data, handled missing values, and performed feature engineering to identify key price determinants.

Model Development

Implemented ensemble learning combining LSTM networks, Random Forest, and XGBoost algorithms to capture both temporal patterns and external factors.

Model Evaluation

Achieved 87% accuracy in short-term price predictions (7-14 days) and 76% accuracy for medium-term forecasts (1-3 months).

Deployment

Created an accessible web platform with API integration for real-time price predictions and market insights.
